Eligibility Requirements

To qualify for a refund under this guarantee, you must meet all of the following at the time of your exam:

  1. Active Pro Subscription: You must have maintained an active, personally-purchased Pro subscription (Weekly, Monthly, or Annual) for a minimum of 30 consecutive days before your exam date. Subscriptions purchased through third parties, employers, or promotional giveaways are not eligible.
  2. Exam Ready Certificate: You must have earned the "Exam Ready" certificate within the app before taking your exam. This certificate requires meeting all readiness criteria tracked by the app, including minimum questions answered, accuracy thresholds, study streak, confidence score, practice test completion, and cleared mistakes.
  3. Exam Timing: You must take your official examination within 14 calendar days after earning your Exam Ready certificate.
  4. First Attempt: This guarantee applies to your first attempt at the examination after earning your certificate. One claim per subscription period.
  5. Genuine Preparation: Your in-app study activity must reflect genuine engagement with the material. This includes completing study sessions across multiple days (not concentrated in a single session), spending reasonable time per question, and demonstrating progressive improvement over your study period.

Required Documentation

All of the following must be submitted for your claim to be reviewed:

  1. Official Score Report: A sharable electronic document or photograph of the official score report issued by the examination's administering organization (e.g., NIC, PTCB, ASVAB/DoD, CompTIA). The report must clearly show your full legal name, the exam name, the exam date, and a failing score or result. Self-reported scores, unofficial transcripts, and screenshots of third-party score-tracking websites are not accepted.
  2. Name Verification: The full legal name on your official score report must match the name associated with your App Store or Google Play purchase receipt.
  3. Purchase Receipt: Your most recent subscription payment receipt from Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play), showing the date of purchase, amount, and account holder name.
  4. In-App Certificate Screenshot: A screenshot of your Exam Ready certificate screen within the app, showing the certificate code, date earned, and all criteria marked as complete.
